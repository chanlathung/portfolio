Featured Project

Inspired by personal real-life events, Panacea was developed with the user in mind. Users can create individual cards for special loved-ones and store doctor's appointment information. Users will fill out a form with the doctor's name, phone number, appointment date and time, and the location of the office. The design for User-Interface (UI) and User-Experience (UX) aimed to provide users with a straight-forward easy-to-use interface without a lot of noise.

I developed this app using Python, Django, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, and jQuery

See it live: Panacea.
