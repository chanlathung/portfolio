Projects.

Recipe-builder

Recipe-builder is an app that another developer and I built using NodeJS, Express, and Mongoose. The app was inspired by our love of cooking and passion to put our own twists on our favorite recipes. Users can create an account and store recipes, also, recipes can be modified by adding, editing, and deleting ingredients. Try me!

Panacea

Panacea was inspired by real-life events and the need to access medical appointment data quickly and hassle-free. The app allows users to consolidate important doctor's appointment information for themselves and others. Due to the nature of sensitive information, cautionary steps to authenticate users are implemented on the back-end using Django authenicators. The app was built using HTML, CSS, Python, Django, and Boostrap.

VR Games

This project was a collaboration between a UX Designer, another developer and I. It was a two-day project that allowed us to leverage our skills and knowledge that we had learned throughout the course to concept an idea and vision and turn it into a product in a very short time-frame. The technologies used were Python, Django, Bootstrap, and Figma, and the application has a functioning back-end.
